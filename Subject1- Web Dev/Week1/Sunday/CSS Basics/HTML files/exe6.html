Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup white flour (140 g)
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder (5 g)
  • 1 bag of vanilla sugar (10 g)
    *Or 1 teaspoon vanilla extract + 1 spoon sugar
  • 2 spoons sugar (25 g)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 spoons oil
  • 1 cup milk (240 ml)
    *For a thicker batter, use Â¾ cup
  • A pinch of salt (from the salt shaker)

The process:

Mix all ingredients well in a bowl with a whisk until you get a smooth batter. Grease a pancake pan or regular pan with non-stick coating and when the pan is hot, pour the batter into the pan using a ladle (of soup). After about a minute, when small bubbles appear on the top of the pancake, turn the side and leave for another 1/2 minute.
Serving suggestion: Recommended serving with chocolate/maple syrup.
